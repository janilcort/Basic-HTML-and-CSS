"The Quiet Hour"

When twilight falls on fields of gold,
And stories soften, sweetly told,

The hush of dusk, a velvet sound,
Wraps weary hearts the world around.

A single star begins to gleam,
Like hope within a half-lost dream,
And night, with gentle, silent art,
Restitches seams of every heart.
